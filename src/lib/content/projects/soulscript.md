SoulScript is an end-to-end AI product for mental wellness: part companion, part reflection engine, part tooling layer for better self-understanding.

## What I Built

The core system combines real-time conversational avatars with Gemini's native audio APIs and emotion-aware music generation through Lyria v1-alpha. The product was built to handle more than 1K concurrent users, so the challenge was not just making the AI respond, but making it feel live under load.

## Persona and Reflection Layer

I built a RAG-powered Persona Dashboard and Reflections agent that synthesizes journal entries and conversational history against psychological literature. The goal was to move beyond a chat surface into a system that can form useful, inspectable user personas with high accuracy.

## Platform Pieces

SoulScript also included automated reports through Google ADK, a community forum, therapist locator flows, and deployment on Google Cloud Platform.
