This book rewired how I think about data systems. Kleppmann doesn't just explain how databases work — he explains *why* they make the tradeoffs they do, and when those tradeoffs will bite you.

The chapter on stream processing alone is worth the price. If you're building anything that handles data at scale, this is the book.
