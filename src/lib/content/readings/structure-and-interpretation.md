SICP isn't about Scheme. It's about what computation *is*. Every abstraction you use daily — closures, iterators, lazy evaluation — gets built from scratch here, and you understand it differently afterward.

Dense, rewarding, humbling. The kind of book that makes you realize how much you don't know.
